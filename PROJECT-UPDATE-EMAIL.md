## What is Cloudflare Pages?

**Cloudflare Pages** is an enterprise-grade hosting platform that delivers your website through a global network of 300+ data centers.

### Key Benefits:
- **Speed:** Content is served from the closest server to your customers
- **Reliability:** 100% uptime guarantee, DDoS protection included
- **Security:** Free SSL/HTTPS, automatic security updates
- **Developer-Friendly:** Automatic deployments from GitHub
- **Cost:** Free for unlimited requests, ~$0.15/month for your traffic level
