From Berlin
to Bengaluru

A journey of authentic German baking traditions, handcrafted daily in the heart of India

By Bäcker & Charlie / Cox Town, Bangalore

Read the Full Story

Every morning at 4 AM, when Bangalore is still asleep, our ovens come alive. The scent of fresh bread fills Cox Town's streets—a smell that transcends borders, cultures, and time zones. This is more than a bakery. It's a bridge between two cities, two continents, two worlds.

Traditional German baking isn't just about following recipes. It's about understanding the soul of the dough, the rhythm of fermentation, the patience required for perfection. Every pretzel we twist, every Brötchen we shape, carries with it centuries of Berlin craftsmanship.

"We don't just bake bread. We bake memories, traditions, and the warmth of home."

— Bäcker & Charlie
