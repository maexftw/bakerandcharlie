Meet Bäcker & Charlie

Bäcker & Charlie is more than just a name – it's an idea.

"Bäcker" means baker in German, reflecting our commitment to authentic German baking traditions.

"Charlie" represents a bear, symbolizing Berlin's spirit of friendship, warmth, and connection.

Our Mission

We aim to bring people together with honest food and an authentic café experience that keeps them coming back for the unique taste they can only find here.
