Our Story

Meet Bäcker & Charlie

“Bäcker” is the baker. “Charlie” is the bear from the Berlin coat-of-arms. Together, we are a bakery that fuses meticulous German craft with the warmth of Bengaluru hospitality. Every loaf, pretzel twist, Berliner fill, and cold brew pour is done in-house, by humans, with time as the secret ingredient.

Our mission

Bread that builds community

Our mission is to be the shared table for the neighborhood — where software engineers meet chai regulars, where travelers taste Berlin, and where the breeze smells like caramelizing pretzels every single morning.
